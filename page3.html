Our Therapists

Matthew Klayman, PT, DPT, OCS

Matthew Klayman is a Physical Therapist joining us from Philadelphia, PA where he worked as a Clinic Supervisor at a large scale private orthopedic practice. He attended Pennsylvania State University where he earned his Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology before attending Rutgers University where he graduated with his Doctorate in Physical Therapy in 2012. Since that time, Dr. Klayman has grown a passion for treating orthopedic and athletic injuries with a specialization in the treatment of lower body injuries and runners. He was heavily involved in the running community in Philadelphia as well; giving multiple lectures and presentations on injury prevention and running mechanics. He looks forward to growing new partnerships with local clubs and offices in the Chicagoland area.

Dr. Klayman believes in the power of manual therapy and the use of a comprehensive full body approach in the treatment of his patients. He has recently earned numerous certifications including becoming a Board Certified Orthopedic Clinical Specialist (OCS). This specialization provides formal recognition for Physical Therapists with advanced clinical knowledge, experience, and skills in the treatment of the orthopedic population. Along with this, Dr. Klayman holds numerous accolades including being a Manual Therapy Certified Therapist and Barefoot Rehab Specialist. He also recently obtained his FMT Basic Kinesiology Taping Certification along with a level 1 certification from SFMA.
